Help & About


ZCalculator is a modern, sleek calculator application built using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, offering a user-friendly interface and functionality.

Features:

  • Basic arithmetic operations
  • Percentage calculation
  • Bracket support
  • History of calculations
  • Theme selection

How to use:
  • Click on the buttons to perform calculations
  • Click on the history button to view the history of calculations
  • Click on the clear button to clear the screen
  • Click on the clear history button to clear the history
  • Click on the theme dropdown to change the theme
